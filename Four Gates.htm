Explanation

The ארבעה שערים ("Four gates") is a shortcut to calculating the year's calendar. It was already referred to in the time of the Gaonim. Once you have found this year's molad for Tishrei, instead of calculating next year's molad and checking the Four Dechiyos for each of them, this chart just tells you the answer: which one of the fourteen calendars is the one for this year.

You need to know
(A) this year's molad Tishrei, and
(B) which of last year, this year, and next year are leap years. There are four possibilities (שערים, "gates"):

  1. Last year was a leap year, this year and next year are not (מפ"פ).
  2. Both last year and next year are leap years, this year is not (מפ"מ).
  3. Next year was a leap year but this year and last year are not (פפ"מ).
  4. This year is a leap year, last year and next year are not (פמ"פ).

The leap years and regular years go in a 19 year cycle ('גו"ח אדז"ט'); the chart shows which years fall into each of the שערים. For instance, מפ"פ applies to years 1 4 9 12 15.

The time of the molad of Tishrei can fall anywhere in the week (7d 0h 0ch → 6d 23h 1079ch). If you would move it through the week from beginning to end, you could calculate the molad of the next Tishrei as well and work out the dechiyos. For small changes, mostly the days of Rosh Hashanah would not change. However, at certain times one of the molads would cross a critical line. At that point, the calendar has a sudden change. Then it will stay the same for a while again, until another critical line is reached.

Those critical points are on the left of the chart. If the molad reaches one of those values, we get a new calendar, and it will stay that way till the molad reaches the next critical point.

The critical points may be for only some of the שערים. To see which ones, extend the line above that value, visually to the right; the places where the line appears are the שערים for which this critical point marks a change.

For instance, for 2d 18h 0ch the line above those numbers is only visible on the right two columns, פפ"מ and פמ"פ: look to the right to see the line begin there. For those two the calendar changes when the molad is ≥ 2d 18h 0ch. (For the left two columns the change had already happened at 2d 15h 589ch.)

If you hover over any of the critical change times on the left, you will see an explanation of that change.
